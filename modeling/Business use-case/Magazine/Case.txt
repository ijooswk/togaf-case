Case

High-level:
  We have moderator, author, reader as users for the magazine. Authors write article and multiple article can be a single magazine by moderator.
  The moderator can allow the magazine to have a comment from reader and reader can read the magazine once it's published.

Use cases:
  1. Author will receive the email for the request to write the article
  2. Moderator can publish the magazine and allow readers to comment on an article
  3. Comments will be part of article as they will be attached below the bottom of article
